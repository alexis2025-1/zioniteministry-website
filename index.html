About Us

Our Mission

Zionite Ministry International is dedicated to spreading the word of God and helping people grow in their faith. We are committed to serving our community and sharing the love of Christ with everyone.

Located in Asante Mampong opposite Maternity, our church provides a welcoming environment for worship and spiritual development.

We believe in the power of prayer,the Prophetic, the importance of fellowship, and the transformative power of God's grace.
